About TrimLab

Founded in 2010, TrimLab has established itself as the premier destination for men's grooming in the city. Our journey began with a simple mission: to revive the traditional barbering experience with modern techniques and premium service.

Our founders, Marcus Johnson and Carlos Rodriguez, both master barbers with over 20 years of combined experience, envisioned a space where men could relax, rejuvenate, and leave looking their absolute best.

At TrimLab, we believe in the artistry of barbering. Each of our barbers is carefully selected for their technical skills, artistic vision, and commitment to excellence. We invest in continuous training to ensure our team stays at the forefront of men's grooming trends and techniques.

Our shop features a premium environment with vintage-inspired decor, comfortable leather chairs, and a welcoming atmosphere that makes every visit an experience to remember.
